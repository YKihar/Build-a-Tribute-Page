Marie Curie

Timeline of Achievements

  • 1867 - Born in Warsaw, Poland
  • 1891 - Moved to Paris to study at Sorbonne
  • 1893 - Earned degree in Physics
  • 1895 - Married Pierre Curie
  • 1898 - Discovered Polonium and Radium
  • 1903 - First Nobel Prize in Physics
  • 1911 - Second Nobel Prize in Chemistry
  • 1934 - Passed away from radiation exposure
